FRANKLIN CITY

Franklin City located on the Brightston mountain range is one of three cities (Franklin, Greenvilee and Springfield) in the region that boasts some of the most breath taking views with alot of activities for the family.

Franklin's freshwater lake provides the idle destination for those that seek fishing, jet sking, tubing or just the simple sand castle building for the family on the sandy shore.

With a rich history of the area being settled in the 1800's you can vist the old town and see the old architectural town or visit the art galleries. Franklin offer also walking trails for beginners to pros as you walk the old pioneer tracks down to the beach where they sailed in on boats.
